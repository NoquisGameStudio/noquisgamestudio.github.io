INUIT beta is now OUT!

Inuit is a serious game about Inuit culture that has just launched its beta version. It offers players a unique educational experience to learn about the history, culture, and lifestyle of Inuit indigenous peoples, with the opportunity to explore the natural environment and learn about hunting, fishing, clothing and toolmaking, as well as music, art, and spirituality. Inuit is an exciting way to learn and have fun while experiencing a fascinating culture.
